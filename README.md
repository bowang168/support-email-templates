## Why This Exists

Most support teams either have no templates (every engineer writes differently) or have bloated ones full of corporate filler that customers don't read.

These templates are:
- **Concise** — every word earns its place
- **Consistent** — unified greeting, sign-off, and structure
- **Copy-paste ready** — replace the `[placeholders]` and send
- **Vendor-neutral** — grounded in Linux support, but works for any product

## Quick Start

1. Open [`templates.md`](templates.md) or [read online](https://bowang168.github.io/support-email-templates/)
2. Find your situation in the [Quick Reference](#quick-reference) table
3. Copy the template, replace `[placeholders]`, send
